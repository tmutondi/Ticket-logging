Purpose & Scope

Defines how TechFlow handles support tickets in Freshdesk. Ensures consistent logging, categorization, escalation, and resolution for employees and beta customers.

Ticket Handling Rules

  • Log all tickets in Freshdesk.
  • Acknowledge tickets immediately.
  • Assign correct category and severity.
  • Update status regularly.
  • Communicate progress and resolutions.

Severity & SLA

  • Critical: Response 15 min, resolve <4 hrs
  • High: Response 1 hr, resolve <8 hrs
  • Medium: Response 4 hrs, resolve <24 hrs
  • Low: Response 1 day, resolve <3 days

Escalation Paths

  • Tier 1: Frontline fixes, low/medium tickets
  • Tier 2: Specialist/engineer, high tickets
  • Tier 3: DevOps/Product team, critical tickets

Communication Templates

  • Acknowledgement, In Progress, Resolution, Closure – automated via Freshdesk

Best Practices

  • Double-check severity before assigning SLA.
  • Keep fields updated (status, notes, assignee).
  • Encourage portal usage for visibility.
  • Document resolutions in the knowledge base.
